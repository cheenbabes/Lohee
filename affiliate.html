What is an affiliate program?

In a nutshell, our affiliate program pays you whenever somebody visits our site by clicking on special link and buys a rebozo. We will provide you with a unique link. Furthermore, even if someone initially goes to our site from your link, leaves without buying anything, then comes back later and buys something, you will still get paid. Sound good? Let's get into the details.

FAQs

What is the commission percentage for the affiliate program?

The base commission is 5%.

Do you offer any higher percentages for increased sales, etc?

We do offer custom rates to specific affiliates. Do you have an Instagram with 1,000+ followers? Do you run a doula, birth, or product blog with 1,000+ unique visitors a month? Are you an influential member of the birth community? Do you have your own highly trafficed brick and mortar or online store? If so, please get in touch and we will discuss a custom percentage!

How will I get paid?

We pay our affiliates every month with PayPal Mass Payments. You can expect to receive your payment by the 10th of the following month. For example, you will receive your affiliate payment on May 10th for the month of April.

How does the whole "Common Hop Link" work? Will I get paid even if my referrals don't initially buy?

From the E-Junkie website:

When you click your Hop Link, you will not see your ID anywhere on the seller's page, nor is that necessary to credit you for the sale, and the buyer does not even need to purchase anything immediately after clicking your link. Clicking your Hop Link sets a cookie in the buyer's browser which expires in 6 months, then we redirect them to the seller's page for that link. Regardless of what kind of Hop Link a buyer follows, you will earn the Product Share % on any purchased items that have one, or the Common Share % on any other purchased items, even if they go directly back to the seller's site later without clicking through your link again.
